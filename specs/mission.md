# Project Mission: UI Interrail Travel Journal

## Overview
A visually stunning, single-page interactive travel journal chronicling a summer Interrail trip to Northern Europe. The project combines modern frontend design, sleek layouts, and expressive animations using vanilla HTML, CSS, and GSAP.

## Project Purpose
To capture and share the memories of the summer Interrail journey with family, friends, and the author. It serves as a digital keepsake that documents the route, highlights key destinations, showcase the highlights of the trip, and tells hostel and other useful informations.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Friends, family, and the author (private/personal memory sharing).
- **Secondary:** Other travel enthusiasts interested in Northern European Interrail routes.

## Design Aesthetic & Vibe
- **Design System:** Sleek, high-quality digital minimalism.
- **Theme:** Dark mode by default, inspired by the atmospheric nights of Northern Europe.
- **Color Palette:** Cold blue accents, deep grays, and crisp white typography.
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (such as Inter or Outfit from Google Fonts) to convey cleanliness and modernism.
- **Visuals:** High-contrast photography placeholder grids, elegant iconography, and smooth micro-interactions.
